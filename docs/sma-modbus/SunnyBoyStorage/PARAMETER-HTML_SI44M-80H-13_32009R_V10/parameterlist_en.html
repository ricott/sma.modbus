  • 1-stage load shedding: When the generator is connected, the Sunny Island stops the load shedding. When the loads are to be shed, the multifunction relay is deactivated.
  • 1-stage load shedding or 1st stage with 2-stage load shedding: When the upper SOC threshold is reached, the Sunny Island ends load shedding. When the loads are to be shed, the multifunction relay is deactivated.
