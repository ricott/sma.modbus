This wizard will repair your SMA inverter connection

The app will attempt to locate your device using the SMA speedwire auto-discovery protocol. If your inverter has moved to a new IP address, this process will help find and reconnect to it.
If the device is not automatically detected, you can manually enter the new IP address of your inverter.

NOTE! Make sure your inverter is still accessible on the network and that Modbus is enabled on the device.
